KOSHER HARVEY WALLBANGER CAKE

 4 eggs 
 2 cups sugar 
 3/4 cup orange juice 
 2/3 cup oil 
 1/4 cup vodka 
 1/4 cup amaretto (e.g. Disaranno) 
 2 tsp baking powder 
 2 cups flour 
 1 pkg (3.4 oz) instant vanilla pudding 


 Preheat over to 350F. Grease bundt pan or 9X13 cake pan.

 Put eggs, sugar, OJ, and oil into mixer or bowl. Mix at medium-high until sugar is disolved.

 Add remaining ingredients to mixer, mix at medium until batter forms (some little lumps of pudding are OK). Pour into pan.

 Bake at 350 for 1 hour in bundt pan or  45 minutes to an hour in a 9x13 pan 

 Allow cake to cool for 20 minutes and then remove from pan.

GLAZE
 1 Cup confectionary sugar 
 1 tsp.  amaretto
 1-2 Tbsp. orange juice 
 1-2 Tbsp. vodka 


Mix sugar w/ amaretto and 1 tbsp each OJ and vodka. 
If glaze is too thick, incrementally add equal amts of OJ and vodka until a thin glaze forms.
Pour or brush over cake
